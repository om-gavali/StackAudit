## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

#### Q: Do I need to connect my live Stripe, OpenAI, or GitHub accounts?
**A**: No. StackAudit is built to be completely secure and frictionless. You manually input your seat numbers and plans using our layout forms. We calculate your savings without needing access to your live data.

#### Q: How does the AI generate recommendations?
**A**: We use a deterministic rules engine to calculate all financial changes based on real public pricing data. We then pass these metrics to Gemini to write a readable, personalized action plan for your team.

#### Q: Is my data shared publicly?
**A**: All dashboards start as completely private. If you choose to share your audit with your team or investors, you can generate a public link. This link automatically scrubs your company name and email to protect your privacy.
